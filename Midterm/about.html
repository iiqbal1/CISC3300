Hi, my name is Raimundo Sanchez

I’m a 21 year old college student who is also an aspiring Landscape and Portrait Photographer based in New York.Being born and raised in New York City, I spent almost every day surrounded by fashion, beauty, nature. It has helped me develop an eye for all three niches. My goal is to capture the beauty and elegance of a moment in time to hopefully capture everlasting memories for everyone involve

Where do you take your photos?

My favorite locations to take picture are around Inwood Hill community, Fordham Community and in Central Park. These are the locations that I feel the most connected to because these are the places that I grew up arou

Why are you doing this?

Photography has help my entrepreneur mind by stimulating the creative part of how I think. Taking picture has always been a way for me to express myself. However, the more I take picture the more I notice that I am doing this because I like to tell the world the stories of others and capture the rare moments that people don’t see.

What inspires you?

I get inspired when I see the smile on peoples faces when they notice their own beauty. Also, I love to capture moment that would have been forgotten if they weren’t caught in a photograph.

What camera do you use?

Sony Alpha 5000, 16mm-50mm Lens & Panasonic Lumix FX70
